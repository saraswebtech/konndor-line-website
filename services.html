Shipping Agent Services

At Konndor Line, we provide reliable and professional Shipping Agent services to ensure smooth vessel operations and efficient port management across global trade routes.

As your trusted port representative, we handle every aspect of vessel coordination — from pre-arrival planning to final departure clearance. Our experienced team works closely with port authorities, customs officials, terminal operators, and service providers to ensure full compliance with local regulations while minimizing delays and operational costs. With strong industry relationships and round-the-clock support, we guarantee seamless communication, accurate documentation, and efficient vessel turnaround for every call.

Our Shipping Agent Services Include:

  • Vessel clearance and port documentation
  • Coordination with port and terminal authorities
  • Customs formalities and regulatory compliance
  • Crew change and husbandry services
  • Bunker arrangements and supply coordination
  • Cargo documentation and bill of lading handling
  • Real-time vessel status updates
  • 24/7 operational and emergency support

NVOCC Services

At Konndor Line, we offer comprehensive and cost-effective NVOCC (Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier) services designed to provide flexible, reliable, and globally connected ocean freight solutions.

As a trusted NVOCC partner, we manage end-to-end ocean transportation by issuing our own House Bills of Lading, securing competitive freight rates, and ensuring efficient cargo consolidation. With strong carrier partnerships and an extensive global network, we provide dependable container space allocation, seamless documentation handling, and complete shipment visibility. Our team ensures smooth coordination from origin to destination while maintaining cost efficiency, transparency, and timely delivery.

Our NVOCC Services Include:

  • Issuance of House Bill of Lading (HBL)
  • Competitive ocean freight rate management
  • Full Container Load (FCL) services
  • Less than Container Load (LCL) consolidation
  • Container space booking and allocation
  • End-to-end shipment coordination
  • Real-time cargo tracking and updates
  • Global agent network support
