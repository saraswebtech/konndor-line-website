Our Services

Comprehensive logistics solutions tailored to meet your business needs

Shipping Agent

We specialize in connecting businesses and individuals worldwide with efficient, reliable, and affordable cargo shipping solutions. Our team of experienced professionals ensures seamless coordination across all aspects of shipping.

  • Global network of shipping routes
  • Real-time tracking and updates
  • Professional documentation handling
  • 24/7 customer support

NVOCC (Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier)

As an NVOCC, we offer comprehensive freight forwarding services without owning vessels. We consolidate shipments from multiple shippers to achieve cost-effective shipping solutions.

  • Competitive freight rates
  • Flexible container options
  • Cargo consolidation services
  • Global coverage

Freight Forwarding

Our comprehensive freight forwarding services cover air, sea, rail, and inland transportation. We handle all aspects of cargo movement from origin to destination.

  • Air Freight - Fast and reliable
  • Sea Freight - Cost-effective bulk cargo
  • Rail Freight - Efficient overland transport
  • Inland Freight - Door-to-door delivery

Logistics Activities

End-to-end logistics solutions tailored to your business needs. We manage the complete supply chain process from warehousing to final delivery.

  • Supply chain management
  • Inventory management
  • Distribution services
  • Order fulfillment

Project Cargo

Specialized handling for oversized, heavy, and complex cargo. We have the expertise and equipment to transport project cargo of any magnitude.

  • Heavy lift cargo handling
  • Out-of-gauge cargo solutions
  • Multi-modal transport
  • Specialized equipment

Perishable Goods

Temperature-controlled logistics for perishable items including food, pharmaceuticals, and other sensitive cargo requiring specific temperature conditions.

  • Cold chain management
  • Temperature monitoring
  • Refrigerated transport
  • Compliance with food safety standards

Specialization 40" Reefer

Expert handling of 40-foot refrigerated containers for temperature-sensitive cargo. Our reefer services ensure product integrity throughout the shipping process.

  • Advanced temperature control
  • Real-time temperature monitoring
  • Pre-cooling services
  • Global reefer container availability

Warehousing

Secure and efficient warehousing solutions for your cargo. We provide storage facilities with advanced inventory management systems.

  • Secure storage facilities
  • Inventory management
  • Pick and pack services
  • Cross-docking services

Customs Clearance

Professional customs clearance services to ensure smooth cross-border transactions. We handle all documentation and regulatory requirements.

  • Import/Export documentation
  • Duty and tax calculation
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Trade consulting
